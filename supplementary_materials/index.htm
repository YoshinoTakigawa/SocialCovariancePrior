Here is the supplemental material of our paper "A General Framework for Implicit and Explicit Social Recommendation". We follow the same notations introduced in our paper.

  • Detailed mathematical derivations in Variational Expectation Maximization (VEM)
    • Jensen's inequality
    • E-step: Updating parameters \( \theta' \) in variational distribution \( q(Z | \theta') \)
      • \( \lambda_{U}, \lambda_{V}, \gamma_{U}, \gamma_{V} \) for latent feature vector (multivariate normal distribution)
      • \( \Lambda_{U}, \Lambda_{V} \) for similarity matrix (Wishart distribution)
    • M-step: Updating parameters \( \theta \) in model likelihood \( p(R | \theta) \)
      • \( \sigma_{U}^{2}, \sigma_{V}^{2} \) for \( l2 \) -norm regularization (zero-mean Gaussian spherical distribution)
      • \( \Lambda_{U}, \Lambda_{V} \) for similarity matrix (Wishart distribution)
      • \( \sigma_{R}^{2} \) for rating (normal distribution)
    • Rating prediction \( \hat{R} \)
